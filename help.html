Document Management Systems
Local Body Tax

Document Management Systems for Aurangabad Municiple Corporations corporation is designed and developed by Breakpoint Info Solutions to provide ease of access to scan and store Local body tax (LBT) Delare's invoices to server through android enabled mobile devices.

This application provides the user interface to add dealer's bill name, amount, and add images of multiple documents in one bill.

Start using the following steps.

    Adding dealer

  • 1. this application just click on Add document menu from the footer menu bar

  • 2. Add Dealer name, amount and date of purchase or sale

  • 3. Click on next will let you to the document capturing screen

    • Adding pages to the document

  • 1. Click on capture doc which will start your camera screen

  • 2. Plece camera on document and capture image

  • 3. After capturing image two button visible at the bottom save & cancel

  • 4. Click on save button this will save the first page of dealers bill

  • 5. Repeat above steps for as many pages as you have in dealer's bill

  • 6. After all the pages has been captured, click on save button to save the dealer document.
